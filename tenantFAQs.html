Tenant FAQs

Answers to commonly asked questions

How do I pay rent?
Rent payment methods are determined by the Landlord. Tenants may be asked to pay via e-wallet or cash as requested.
How do I submit a maintenance request?
Log into the tenant portal and click "Submit Maintenance Request." Provide a detailed description of the issue and send photos to the Landlord. For emergencies (flooding, no heat, gas leaks), call our emergency line immediately.
What is the guest policy?
All guests must comply with the Landlord’s guest policy. Please refer to your agreement for specific rules regarding overnight visitors and visiting hours.
Can I have pets?
Pets are welcome only with the Landlord's express consent. Please check with us regarding pet eligibility and any applicable fees or deposits that may apply.
What happens if I pay rent late?
Late fees depend on what was agreed upon with your landlord. Please check your contract or get in touch with the landlord for more information.
Am I allowed to make changes to the unit?
"Please consult your contract before making any changes to the apartment. All modifications, including painting or hardware installation, require prior approval from the Landlord.
