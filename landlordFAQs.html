Landlord FAQs

Management resources and owner information

How do I list my property?
To list your property, simply navigate to the "Add Property" section of your dashboard. You will need to provide the unit address, monthly rent amount, a clear description of the amenities, and high-quality photos. Once submitted, your listing will be visible to potential tenants seeking a new home.
How are tenants screened?
Tenant screening is a vital step in ensuring a safe community. The process typically involves verifying the applicant’s identity, employment status, and rental history. Landlords may also request character references to ensure the applicant aligns with the building’s community standards.
When and how do I receive rent payments?
Rent is due based on your agreed-upon start date. Tenants can pay you via cash or e-wallet (GCash/Maya) or cash payment as arranged with you landlord. A receipt is required for every transaction to ensure your records are accurate.
How are maintenance repairs handled?
Maintenance requests should be submitted directly to the landlord or via the official maintenance request. For urgent issues (like plumbing leaks or electrical faults), please contact the landlord immediately. Minor cosmetic changes or modifications to the unit require prior written approval from the landlord.
What is the process for late payments?
If rent is not received by the end of the agreed-upon grace period, a late fee may be applied as outlined in the lease agreement. Tenants should contact their landlord immediately if they anticipate a delay to discuss potential extensions or payment plans.
