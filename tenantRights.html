Tenant Rights & Guidelines

💳 Rent & Payments

Rent is due based on your agreed-upon start date. Payments are accepted via E-Wallet (GCash/Maya) or Cash as arranged with the Landlord. Always ensure a digital or physical receipt is generated for every transaction to protect your payment history.

🛠️ Unit Modifications

Your unit is your home, but permanent modifications or changes depend strictly on your contract terms. You must contact the Landlord and receive written approval before painting, drilling, or altering the unit in any way.

🐾 Pets & Guests

Permission for pets and policies regarding overnight guests are at the Landlord's discretion. Please consult your lease agreement for specific rules regarding additional pet fees or visitor duration limits.

⚠️ Late Fees

In the event of delayed payment, late fees will be applied according to the terms of your signed contract. If you anticipate a delay, please communicate with the Landlord immediately to discuss a resolution.

Important: These guidelines are designed to ensure a safe and harmonious community for everyone. When in doubt, always refer to your signed Lease Agreement or contact the Landlord directly.
